Tutorials

Our tutorials will guide you through various tasks within the Student Data Portal:

  • Tutorial : Introduction to the Portal

  1. Step 1: Login to the Website

    • Open your web browser and go to [website URL].
    • Enter your username and password to log in.

  2. Step 2: Navigate to the Student Management Section

    • Once logged in, you will see the dashboard.

  3. Step 3: Add a New Student

    • Fill in the required information, such as student name, date of birth, address, etc.
    • Upload any necessary documents (if applicable).
    • Click the "Save" button to add the new student.

  4. Step 4: Edit Student Information (if needed)

    • Go back to the Student Management section.
    • Locate the student record you want to edit and follow the steps to make changes.
    • Click "Save" to apply the changes.

  5. Step 5: Logout

    • Always remember to log out when you've finished using the website.
    • Click on the "Logout" button located in the top-right corner.
